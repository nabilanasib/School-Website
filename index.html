About us: Sofia Private School practices a broad education system that promotes active learning and advancement in academic, social, and physical spheres. The school serves children grades K-6 and we offer a pre-school program. We believe in taking a holistic appraoch to learning, thus we revise our curriculm annually to fit the needs of the students and the environment. Our teachers and students come from all background and all walks of life to create a diverse teaching and learning community.
